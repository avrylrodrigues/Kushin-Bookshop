The Silent Patient

The story of Alicia Berenson, a renowned painter who shoots her husband, Gabriel, five times in the face and then stops speaking. The narrative is primarily told through the perspective of Theo Faber, a psychotherapist who becomes obsessed with Alicia's case. Despite her refusal to speak, Theo is determined to uncover the truth behind Alicia's actions and break through her silence. s Theo delves deeper into Alicia's past and psyche, he unravels shocking secrets.The novel explores themes of trauma, guilt, and the complexity of the human mind
