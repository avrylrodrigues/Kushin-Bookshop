The Hunger Games

Set in a dystopian future, Katniss Everdeen volunteers to take her sister's place in a brutal televised event where teenagers fight to the death. Alongside Peeta Mellark, her fellow tribute, Katniss navigates the deadly arena, confronting moral dilemmas and the oppressive Capitol. As the Games progress, Katniss and Peeta form alliances, defy the odds, and challenge the system, sparking a revolution that reverberates throughout Panem. Through sacrifice and resilience, Katniss becomes a symbol of hope for the oppressed districts, igniting a rebellion against the Capitol's tyranny.
