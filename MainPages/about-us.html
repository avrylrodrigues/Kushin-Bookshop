Company Information

Kushin Bookshop was founded in 2000 by Hayate Ichikura and Motoharu Igarashi with a simple yet profound vision: to create a haven for book lovers and knowledge seekers. What started as a modest bookstore tucked away in a quaint corner of Nakano Ward of Tokyo has since blossomed into a beloved literary institution within the community. From its inception, Kushin Bookshop has been committed to curating a diverse and eclectic collection of books spanning various genres, from classic literature to contemporary bestsellers, from thought-provoking non-fiction to enchanting children's tales. Every title on our shelves is carefully selected to inspire, educate, and entertain readers of all ages and interests. Over the years, Kushin Bookshop has evolved into more than just a place to purchase books. It has become a cultural hub—a gathering place where bookworms, writers, and intellectuals alike converge to engage in lively discussions, attend author readings, and participate in literary events. Despite the ever-changing landscape of the publishing industry, Kushin Bookshop remains steadfast in its commitment to promoting literacy, fostering a love for reading, and supporting local authors and artists. Our dedicated staff members are passionate about connecting readers with the perfect book and providing exceptional customer service with a personal touch. As Kushin Bookshop continues to write its story, our mission remains unchanged: to be a beacon of literary enlightenment and a cherished destination for book enthusiasts far and wide.

Mission Statement

At Kushin Bookshop, we are dedicated to fostering a love for literature and knowledge within our community. We strive to provide a diverse selection of books that inspire, educate, and entertain readers of all ages. Through personalized service and a welcoming atmosphere, we aim to be a cherished destination where every visitor can discover the joy of reading and the power of imagination.

Vision Statement

Our vision at Kushin Bookshop is to be the premier hub for literary exploration and cultural enrichment. We aspire to cultivate a vibrant literary ecosystem where authors, readers, and thinkers can come together to engage in meaningful conversations, exchange ideas, and celebrate the written word. With innovation, passion, and integrity, we aim to continuously elevate the book-buying experience and make a lasting impact on the communities we serve.
